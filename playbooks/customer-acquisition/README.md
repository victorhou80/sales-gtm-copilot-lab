# Customer Acquisition System Kit

This kit packages a practical plan for selling digital products, templates, and small tools by building a simple customer acquisition system first.

## Name

Customer Acquisition System Kit

中文名：获客系统启动包

## Core Thesis

Do not start with a large SaaS product. Start with a narrow customer group, a painful money-adjacent problem, a visible sample, and a lightweight offer:

```text
Target customer
  -> content / outreach / search entry
  -> free sample, checklist, calculator, or demo
  -> lead capture
  -> follow-up
  -> paid digital product
  -> higher-ticket setup, diagnosis, or coaching
  -> referral, case study, repeat purchase
```

The best starting format is:

```text
Niche digital package + spreadsheet/tool + optional done-with-you service
```

## Recommended First Product

Start with one specific industry and sell a package that helps them get more leads and avoid underpricing.

Example:

```text
30-Day Local Service Growth Kit:
content calendar + DM conversion scripts + package pricing calculator + lead tracker
```

Good niches:

- Nail salons
- Pet grooming stores
- Photography studios
- Fitness coaches
- Home repair or renovation teams
- Freelance designers
- Small agencies

## Fast Start

1. Pick one niche, not a broad market.
2. Create a free lead magnet: sample checklist, 3-day content plan, or calculator.
3. Build a simple product page with screenshots and a clear delivery list.
4. Track leads in the provided `templates/lead-tracker.csv`.
5. Send 100-200 targeted messages in 14 days.
6. Publish 30-60 short posts in the first month.
7. Sell a paid package at 99-299 CNY or 19-49 USD.
8. Upsell a setup, diagnosis, or coaching package at 999-2999 CNY.

## Success Criteria

Use payment behavior, not likes, as the main validation signal.

| Metric | Healthy Signal |
|---|---:|
| Product page purchase conversion | 1%+ |
| Free sample to paid conversion | 3%+ |
| Refund rate | Below 8% |
| Pro-tier share | 25%+ |
| Support time per order | Below 20 minutes |
| First 14-day paid orders | 3-10 orders |

## Important Notes

- Avoid promising guaranteed revenue, followers, clients, jobs, or investment outcomes.
- Avoid selling copyrighted, pirated, leaked, scraped, or unlicensed materials.
- Keep tax, data, and content compliance simple at the beginning.
- For the US, a Merchant of Record platform plus Schedule C bookkeeping is often the lowest-friction start.
- For mainland China, sustained sales should generally move toward an individual business registration, small-scale taxpayer setup, compliant merchant collection, and regular filing.
