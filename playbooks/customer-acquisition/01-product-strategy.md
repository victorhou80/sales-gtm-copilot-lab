# Product Strategy

## Final Recommendation

The most practical first product is not a pure PDF, not a full SaaS app, and not a generic prompt bundle. The strongest starting product is:

```text
Niche audience + money-adjacent pain + digital toolkit + spreadsheet/small tool + optional service
```

Recommended first offer:

```text
Local Service Growth and Pricing Toolkit
```

Example title:

```text
Nail Salon 30-Day Xiaohongshu Growth Kit:
content ideas, shooting scripts, DM replies, package pricing calculator, and lead tracker
```

## Why This Direction Wins

It is close to money:

- More leads
- More appointments
- Better follow-up
- Less underpricing
- Clearer profit margins

It is easy to accept:

- No system migration
- Works with Excel, Feishu, Google Sheets, Notion, PDF, or a simple webpage
- Can be used immediately
- Screenshots and samples make the value visible

It is easy to promote:

- Before/after content works well
- Free samples can be shared
- Users can show their result
- Search-style content has clear keywords

## MVP Scope

For the first product, keep the package small but concrete.

Include:

- 30-day content calendar
- 20 headline examples
- 10 shooting or post scripts
- DM reply templates
- Package pricing and profit calculator
- Lead tracker
- 5-minute screen-recorded walkthrough
- Free sample

Exclude:

- Full SaaS login system
- Complicated automation
- Broad all-industry playbooks
- Unrealistic income promises
- Heavy consulting bundled into the low-price product

| Pro | 199-299 CNY | Main revenue | Full templates, examples, videos, updates |
| Bundle | 399-699 CNY | Higher ticket | Pro + group Q&A or one review |

| Team/Enterprise | 9800+ CNY/year | Scale | Training, licenses, invoices, internal templates |

## Product Validation Rules

Continue if:

- 14 days brings 3-10 paid orders.
- At least 20 people have serious conversations.
- Buyers clearly understand the delivery.
- Refund rate is below 8%.
- Some users ask for customization or coaching.

Adjust if:

- People like posts but do not ask for the sample.
- Sample users do not buy.
- Buyers only choose the lowest price.
- Support takes more than 30 minutes per order.
- Users ask "what do I actually get?"

## Avoid

- Generic prompt collections
- Generic Notion life-management templates
- Medical, investment, legal, migration, or job-guarantee promises
- Copyrighted, leaked, pirated, scraped, or unlicensed materials
- Starting with a full SaaS before demand is validated
