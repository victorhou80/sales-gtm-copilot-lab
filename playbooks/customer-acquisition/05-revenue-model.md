# Revenue Model

## Recommended Revenue Path

The safest path is:

```text
One-time digital product
  -> template + diagnosis/setup service
  -> membership or subscription
  -> team/enterprise license
  -> affiliate/ads as side income
```

Do not start with a forced subscription. Subscription only works when users receive continuous value.

## Refund Reserve

Reserve cash for refunds.

| Product Type | Reserve |
|---|---:|
| Simple templates | 2-6% |
| High-promise courses | 8-15% |
| Small tools | 3-8% |
| Setup/coaching | 2-10% |
| Enterprise | 0-3% |

## Pricing Rules

- Do not price too low. Extremely low prices attract low-quality support demand.
- Overseas products should generally be at least 15 USD when using platforms with fixed transaction fees.
- Keep the delivery list clear to reduce refunds.
- Use previews and samples before purchase.
- Define support scope clearly.

## Upgrade Strategy

After purchase, ask:

```text
Do you want me to help adapt this to your business?
```

Offer:

- Template diagnosis
- Pricing-table setup
- Content calendar customization
- DM script rewrite
- One-hour review
- Group Q&A

Standardize service boundaries:

- Fixed duration
- Fixed deliverables
- Fixed revision count
- Deposit before work
- Written acceptance criteria

## Subscription Criteria

Only create a membership if at least one continuous value exists:

- Monthly new templates
- Updated platform rules
- Case-study library
- Tool credits
- Office hours
- Community review
- Industry database

Healthy membership metrics:

- Monthly churn below 8%
- Annual plan share above 30%
- Membership revenue 20-40% of total revenue
