# Promotion Playbook

## Promotion Principle

Digital products are hard to sell because buyers fear buying "air." Promotion must make the value visible.

Use:

- Before/after comparison
- Real screenshots
- Free sample
- Clear delivery list
- User result
- Small trial

Avoid:

- "100GB resources"
- "Secret internal material"
- "Guaranteed income"
- Overly broad claims
- No screenshots or samples

## Content Hook Library

Use these patterns:

```text
I reduced [task] from 3 hours to 12 minutes.
Before vs after: same [quote/page/script], different result.
Do not buy another course before checking this template.
New [niche] owners usually miss these 8 items.
I made a 30-day [niche] content calendar.
If customers ask price but do not book, check these 3 replies.
This pricing table shows your minimum profitable price.
Comment "[keyword]" and I will send the sample.
```

## Xiaohongshu / Short Video Script

```text
0-3 sec:
Are you stuck on [specific pain]?

3-10 sec:
I made a [toolkit] that breaks the workflow into simple steps.

10-25 sec:
Demo: input [material], choose [scenario], get [result].

25-35 sec:
Here is before, here is after, and here is the delivery list.

35-45 sec:
Comment [keyword] and I will send the free sample.
It is for [target audience], not for [non-fit audience].
```

## WeChat Group Cold Start

```text
I am testing a small [toolkit] this week. It is not a big course.

I want to find 10 real users to try the free sample. The only request is 3 sentences of feedback after using it.

It is suitable for [audience 1] and [audience 2]. It is not suitable for [non-fit audience].

If useful, I will send the demo and answer questions tonight.
```

## DM Follow-Up Script

```text
I sent the free sample.

Which part is harder for you right now:
1. getting more inquiries
2. converting inquiries into appointments
3. pricing packages without losing profit
```

If they reply:

```text
That is exactly what the full kit is built for.

It includes [delivery list]. Basic is [price], Pro is [price], and Bundle is [price].

I can send screenshots and the directory first, so you can decide whether it fits your situation.
```

## Paid Ads Rule

Only run ads after organic content proves demand.

Ads are allowed if:

- Organic posts generate comments, saves, or DMs.
- Product page conversion is 3-5% or higher.
- You have real buyers.
- Refund rate is low.
- You know which niche and hook convert.

Stop ads if:

- Cost per purchase exceeds 30-40% of gross margin.
- Buyers ask what the product actually includes.
- There is no upsell or repeat purchase.
