# 14-Day Launch Plan

## Goal

Launch a narrow digital toolkit and get the first 3-10 paid orders within 14 days.

Do not judge the launch by likes. Judge it by real conversations and payment.

## Day-by-Day Plan

| Day | Task | Output |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Pick one niche | One target customer and one painful use case |
| 2 | Interview or research 10 target customers | Pain list, objections, language |
| 3 | Build a free lead magnet | Sample checklist, sample content calendar, or calculator |
| 4 | Build the lead tracker | Spreadsheet ready for daily use |
| 5 | Draft the paid product | Delivery list and first version outline |
| 6 | Create core templates | Spreadsheet, scripts, checklist, examples |
| 7 | Create product page | Screenshots, price, FAQ, delivery list |
| 8 | Add payment and delivery | Platform link or manual payment workflow |
| 9 | Publish 3 posts | Test hooks |
| 10 | DM or contact 30 target users | First conversations |
| 11 | Follow up and revise page | Improve based on questions |
| 12 | Publish 3 more posts and contact 30 more users | More sample requests |
| 13 | Offer early-bird pricing | First 3-10 paid customers |
| 14 | Review metrics and decide | Continue, reposition, or rebuild |

## 14-Day Targets

| Metric | Target |
|---|---:|
| Target users contacted | 100-200 |
| Free sample requests | 50-100 |
| Serious conversations | 20 |
| Paid orders | 3-10 |
| Refund rate | Below 8% |
| Upgrade interest | 2+ prospects |

## Product Page Checklist

The page should answer:

- What problem does this solve?
- Who is this for?
- Who is this not for?
- What exactly is included?
- What does the buyer receive immediately?
- What does it look like?
- How long does setup take?
- What support is included?
- What is the refund rule?
- How can the buyer upgrade?


## Decision Rules

Continue if:

- People understand the offer quickly.
- At least 3 buyers pay.
- Buyer questions repeat, meaning the market is legible.
- Some users ask for customization.

Reposition if:

- Many people ask for the sample but nobody buys.
- Buyers only care about the cheapest tier.
- The niche has no money or no urgent pain.

Stop or rebuild if:

- You cannot find 100 target prospects.
- You cannot produce a visible result screenshot.
- Users do not believe the product can help.
- The product requires heavy one-on-one support for every buyer.
