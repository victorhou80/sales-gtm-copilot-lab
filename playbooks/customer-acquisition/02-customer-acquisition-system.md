# Customer Acquisition System

## Definition

A customer acquisition system is not just software. It is a repeatable process that turns strangers into leads and leads into paying customers.

```text
Target customer
  -> content / outreach / search
  -> free sample or demo
  -> contact or lead capture
  -> follow-up
  -> paid product
  -> higher-ticket offer
  -> referral and repeat purchase
```

## Minimum Viable System

| Module | Purpose | Simple Tool |
|---|---|---|
| Target customer list | Know who to contact | Excel, Feishu, Google Sheets |
| Content entry | Let strangers discover you | Xiaohongshu, Douyin, Zhihu, WeChat groups, Reddit, LinkedIn |
| Free lead magnet | Create trust and capture intent | Sample, checklist, calculator, demo |
| Product page | Explain what buyers get | Notion, Feishu doc, Carrd, Framer |
| Lead tracker | Avoid losing prospects | Feishu Base, Airtable, spreadsheet |
| Follow-up scripts | Turn questions into orders | WeChat quick replies, email templates |
| Payment and delivery | Collect money and deliver files | Xiaoe-tech, Youzan, WeChat Store, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy |
| Referral loop | Get repeat purchase and referrals | Coupon, commission, case-study campaign |

## Example Funnel

Product:

```text
Nail Salon 30-Day Growth and Pricing Kit
```

Funnel:

```text
Post on Xiaohongshu:
"Stop posting random salon content. I made a 30-day appointment content calendar."
  -> Comment or DM keyword: "nail"
  -> Send free 3-day sample
  -> Add to lead tracker
  -> Ask whether the main issue is traffic, appointment conversion, or pricing
  -> Send product page
  -> Sell Basic, Pro, or Bundle
  -> Follow up after 3 days
  -> Upsell setup or coaching
```

## The Five Assets You Need

### 1. Free Lead Magnet

Good examples:

- 7-day Xiaohongshu content calendar
- Pricing profit calculator
- Client follow-up checklist
- AI usage risk self-check
- 3 sample DM reply scripts

Rules:

- Small but specific
- Useful within 5 minutes
- Shows the style of the paid product
- Does not feel like a generic file dump

### 2. Product Page

Must include:

- Pain scenario
- Who it is for
- Who it is not for
- Delivery list
- Screenshots or sample
- Price ladder
- Refund and support rules

### 3. Lead Tracker

Use the included `templates/lead-tracker.csv`.

Minimum fields:

- Date
- Source
- Name
- Niche
- Pain
- Lead magnet sent
- Status
- Intent level
- Order amount
- Next follow-up date

### 4. Follow-Up Scripts

Initial reply:

```text
I will send it to you. This sample is made for [niche]. It includes [specific sample].

Which part is harder for you right now: getting traffic, converting inquiries, or pricing packages?
```

Product-page reply:

```text
Your case sounds like a [specific pain] problem.

The full kit includes [delivery list]. Basic is [price], Pro is [price], and Bundle is [price].

I will send the directory and screenshots first, so you can check whether it fits your business.
```

Second follow-up:

```text
You can try the first sample today.

If you want to save time, the Pro version already includes the full templates, examples, and walkthrough.
```

### 5. Offer Ladder

Do not sell only one price.

| Tier | Price | Role |
|---|---:|---|
| Free | 0 | Lead capture |
| Basic | 99 CNY | First purchase |
| Pro | 199 CNY | Main revenue |
| Bundle | 399 CNY | Higher ticket |
| Setup | 999-2999 CNY | Profit |

## Daily Operating Rhythm

Every day:

```text
Publish 2 short posts
Interact with 20 target customers
Send 5-10 free samples
Follow up with yesterday's leads
Record every lead status
```

Weekly:

```text
Review best-performing hooks
Update product page screenshots
Add common questions to FAQ
Collect testimonials
Improve the Pro version
```

## Main Metrics

Track these:

- Impressions
- Comments or DMs
- Free sample requests
- Product page views
- Purchases
- Refunds
- Upgrade requests
- Referrals

Ignore vanity metrics early. Followers are not the goal. Qualified leads are the goal.
